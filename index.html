Desertflow Global Bundle

Per-region NDVI forecasting across 133 WWF ecoregions and 22 years using Google Earth Engine. The headline contribution is a leakage-aware validation protocol that catches the common failure modes most published NDVI forecasting studies miss — sister vegetation features, shared cross-region statistics, pheno-prior leakage.
